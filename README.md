## Introduction

This is my personal portfolio website where I display my work and skills. It includes details about my projects, achievements, works, me, and my contact information. This site aims to provide potential employers or collaborators with an easy way to see what I can do.

## Features

- **Home:** Introduction and overview of who I am.
- **About:** Background information about me, my skills, and my professional journey.
- **Achievement:** Information about my achievements.
- **Projects:** Detailed descriptions and links to my projects.
- **Publication:** Detailed descriptions and links to my publication.
- **Contact:** A form to get in touch with me.

- HTML5
